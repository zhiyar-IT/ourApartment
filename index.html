Apartment Room Rules

For all 5 friends ‚Äì Management & Cleaning

Rules

  1. After eating, you have to wash the dishes.
  2. Taking shoes to the hall is not allowed.
  3. Making noises after 12 is not allowed. The person who makes the noises will be questioned.
  4. Whenever someone (or a friend of yours) at the apartment is tired and falls asleep, making noises is not allowed. You have to be polite to your friends at all times. This rule is only valid when someone is tired.
  5. Leaving the rubbish behind is not allowed, even for a short time, because the rubbish will pile up and cause viruses and other problems.
  6. Everyone must take their rubbish outside. It is not allowed for anyone to leave rubbish in the hall or anywhere inside.
  7. Do not leave trash on the eating table. This is a red line and not allowed.
  8. After you go to your friends' rooms, it is not allowed to leave your rubbish inside their room. Please obey this rule.
  9. Playing songs with high volume is not allowed when someone is sleeping. This one is important.
  10. The most important part: Clean the WC and the shower whenever you use it.
  11. Do not get so pissed off when something happens you don't like. Do not get mad at your friends; this makes it even worse.
  12. When you sit in the hall after 12 pm, do not make any noises because a person might have slept.
  13. After you wash your dishes, also please clean the sink. Do not leave the sink dirty.
  14. Bringing your friend who others might not know is not allowed.
  15. Only these people can enter the apartment without asking: Ramin, Sherwan, Aras.
  16. When cleaning the rooms, hall, or anywhere, everyone must join to clean together.
  17. Whenever a problem happens between two of you, you must inform the others and solve the problem together.
  18. When someone older than you speaks, you have to respect and obey him.
  19. Obeying these laws shows your home's real face, and not obeying them is the opposite.
